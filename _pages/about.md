I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate at Nankai University. My research focuses on flexible-structure robotic systems (i.e., pneumatic artificial muscle-actuated robots and flexible-joint robots), including hysteresis modeling, motion control, and learning-based compliant interaction. I have published more than 10 peer-reviewed journal papers, primarily in top journals in artificial intelligence and automatic control. Recipient of the National Scholarship for Doctoral Students, the National Scholarship for Master's Students, the Outstanding Master's Thesis Award of Shandong Province, and several other academic awards. Independent reviewer for IEEE TIE, IEEE TMECH, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TSMCS, IEEE TCST, IEEE CDC, etc.

During my Ph.D., I was awarded the Youth Student Basic Research Project (for Ph.D. Students) by National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and the Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program (for Ph.D. Students) by China Association for Science and Technology (CAST).
